# Superseded — retracted positions and why

*status: Superseded. These are **not** current positions. They are recorded so that the earlier public
writings (Hatena / early note / early Zenodo) do not mislead, and so the theory's own revision history is
visible.*
Last updated: 2026-07-04

Recording what was dropped, and why, is not housekeeping. The core criterion is **reversibility / revisability**
(F6): a theory that hid its own corrections would contradict itself. This ledger applies the same
scale-transition constraints to the theory's history — **reversible correction, non-personal memory** — that
the repository's three tiers apply to its structure.

Each entry: the claim as originally made · why it was dropped · what replaced it.

---

## S1. Reversible ontology / "world = the whole brain" / "observer = world" (Hatena, Oct 2025)

**Claim.** The earliest layer (Hatena, 2025-10-14–18) asserts *Reversible Ontology*: the observer and the world
are not separate but identical; "the world is nothing but the whole brain"; ethics, others, and the self are
all brain-constructs; and this is asserted as **"an informational necessity, not a poetic metaphor."**

**Why dropped.** This is **idealism asserted as a necessity**, and it contradicts the frozen core:
- **F2** (methodological realism): the world is real, prior, and shared — *not* "= my brain".
- **F3** (indirect realism, *one-world, not idealism*, + silence): we do not assert "observer = world" as a
  necessity; the ultimate is untouchable, so no such modal/metaphysical fact is claimed.

The move over-asserts a fact that lies beyond the observation limit.

**Defensible kernel retained.** "What we see is the brain's reconstruction" = **indirect realism, already in
F3**. Only the *idealist overshoot* (observer = world = necessity; no mind-independent world) is retracted.

**Replaced by:** F1 (OSR — Ω is a descriptive reading, not a cosmic identity), F2 (posit realism), F3
(indirect realism + silence).

## S2. "Pleasure = Coherence" as an identity (early Hatena / note; retracted in Zenodo v1.2)

**Claim.** Pleasure *is* coherence (P = C).

**Why dropped.** Too strong; it conflates the *signal* with the *structure*.

**Replaced by:** pleasure = dC/dt — a rate / signal, a functional correlate (F8). Coherence is not identical to
pleasure.

## S3. "Value = Ω", "Ω = growth / expansion", "one scale-invariant law" (early; retracted in v1.2)

**Claim.** Value is Ω; Ω is growth / expansion; a single scale-invariant law runs from nature to civilization.

**Why dropped.** Value ≠ Ω (F7: Ω is descriptive, not a value). Growth / expansion can be collapse-ward (cancer,
overshoot). Scale-invariance was overstated.

**Replaced by:** F1 (Ω = descriptive reading), F7 (non-normative). What actually transfers across scales is the
*question-form* and the *reversibility criterion* (F6), not one law or one mechanism.

## S4. Nāgārjuna's emptiness reified into an "Ω coherence field" (Zenodo, Nov 2025)

**Claim.** Śūnyatā = "the invariant principle underlying consciousness" / a cosmic Ω coherence field; a "unified
field theory of consciousness."

**Why dropped.** This **reifies emptiness into a positive ground** — precisely the error Madhyamaka names
(MMK 13.8; *grasping emptiness as a view*). It conflicts with F1 (Ω = a descriptive reading, not a real field).

**Replaced by:** kept as **Companion C1**, explicitly labelled Kant/Yogācāra-flavored (not strict Madhyamaka).
Panpsychism is retained only as *one undecided option* under F8, not as an asserted cosmic fact.

## S5. ZZ integration as a fact achieved by the Buddha (Hatena / note, Oct 2025)

**Claim.** ZZ integration is not an ideal but a fact *actually achieved* by the Buddha.

**Why dropped.** It asserts an inner state that lies **beyond the observation limit** (F3, F8); it is
unverifiable.

**Replaced by:** kept as a phenomenological hypothesis in **Companion C5**; the "achieved fact" assertion is
withdrawn.

## The trajectory this ledger records

The theory **cooled** from bold metaphysics toward disciplined description:

> Oct 2025 (Hatena) — grand monism: world = brain = observer, Ω as a cosmic field, ZZ integration as achieved fact
> → 2025–2026 (Zenodo v1.2–v1.3) — the strong claims dropped; pleasure / value / Ω separated
> → 2026 — pragmatic realism, indirect realism, silence about the ultimate, Ω as a descriptive reading (the frozen core).

That cooling is not a weakness of the project; by the theory's own principle (F6), it *is* the theory working.
